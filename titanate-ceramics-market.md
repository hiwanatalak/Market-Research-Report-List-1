<p><strong>The Titanate Ceramics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oxide</li><li>Non-oxide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Titanate ceramics are categorized into two main market types: oxide and non-oxide. Oxide titanate ceramics, primarily composed of titanium dioxide, exhibit excellent thermal stability, mechanical strength, and electrical properties, making them suitable for applications like capacitors and sensors. Non-oxide titanate ceramics, on the other hand, include materials like zirconate and niobate, known for their superior piezoelectric properties and are commonly used in transducers and actuators. Both categories play crucial roles in various advanced technological applications.</p></p>
