<p><ul><li>Conventional EEG</li><li>Video EEG</li><li>Dynamic EEG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EEG equipment market includes several types catering to different diagnostic needs. Conventional EEG systems primarily capture electrical activity in the brain over a short period, useful for routine assessments. Video EEG combines this technology with continuous video monitoring, allowing for the correlation of seizures with brain activity for more detailed evaluations. Dynamic EEG systems, including portable or ambulatory devices, facilitate long-term monitoring in various settings, providing insights into brain function over extended periods. Each type serves distinct clinical applications.</p></p>
