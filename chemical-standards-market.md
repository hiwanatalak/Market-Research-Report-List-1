<p><strong>The Chemical Standards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Primary Standards"</li><li>"Secondary Standards"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemical standards market comprises primary and secondary standards, each serving distinct purposes. Primary standards are highly pure substances used to calibrate instruments and validate analytical methods, providing a reliable reference point. Secondary standards, on the other hand, are less pure and are typically calibrated against primary standards. They are used in routine analyses within laboratories. Together, these standards ensure accuracy and consistency in chemical measurements, playing a critical role in research, quality control, and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
