<p><ul><li>Impulse Radio</li><li>Multi-band UWB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultra Wideband (UWB) market is primarily categorized into two types: Impulse Radio and Multi-band UWB. Impulse Radio focuses on transmitting short pulses across a wide frequency range, ideal for applications requiring precision, such as location tracking and imaging systems. In contrast, Multi-band UWB utilizes multiple frequency bands for communication, enhancing data throughput and reliability, making it suitable for high-speed data transfer in consumer electronics and automotive sectors. Both types leverage unique properties of UWB technology to meet diverse market needs.</p></p>
