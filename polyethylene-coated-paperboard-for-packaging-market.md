<p><p>Polyethylene Coated Paperboard for Packaging is categorized into two primary types based on the density of polyethylene used: Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) and High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE). </p><p>LDPE offers flexibility and is commonly used in applications requiring light-weight and moisture resistance, making it ideal for packaging food and consumer goods. In contrast, HDPE provides higher rigidity and strength, suitable for products that demand greater durability and protection. Both types enhance the paperboard’s barrier properties, contributing to sustainability in packaging solutions.</p></p>
