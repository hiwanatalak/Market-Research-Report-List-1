<p><p>Vacuum cleaning furnaces are categorized into two primary types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical vacuum furnaces feature a design where components are loaded from the top and are often more space-efficient, making them suitable for smaller facilities. In contrast, horizontal vacuum furnaces have a side-loading design, which allows for easier access and handling of larger components. Both types serve specific applications in material processing, offering varying benefits in terms of design, space utilization, and operational efficiency.</p></p>
