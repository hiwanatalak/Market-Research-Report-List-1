<p><p>The metallised film market comprises several key types, including metallised polycarbonate, polyester, polyethylene, polyimide, and polypropylene. Metallised polycarbonate films offer high clarity and impact resistance, while metallised polyester films are known for their durability and barrier properties. Metallised polyethylene films are lightweight and moisture-resistant, making them ideal for packaging. Metallised polyimide films have excellent thermal stability and are suited for electronics. Lastly, metallised polypropylene films combine flexibility and strength, making them versatile for various applications in packaging and insulation.</p></p>
