<p><p>The area scan camera market primarily consists of two main types: CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device). CMOS cameras are known for their lower power consumption, faster capture rates, and integration capabilities, making them ideal for applications requiring speed and efficiency. Conversely, CCD cameras are recognized for their superior image quality and low noise levels, making them suitable for high-resolution imaging tasks. Both technologies serve diverse industries, including manufacturing, healthcare, and security, each with distinct advantages.</p></p>
