<p><p>Supervisory switches are essential components across various valve types to monitor and enhance safety in fire protection systems. For OS&Y (Outside Screw and Yoke) valves, these switches provide feedback on valve positions, ensuring they are securely shut or open. In butterfly valves, supervisory switches facilitate quick detection of valve status to support efficient system operation. For post indicator valves, they enable remote monitoring, ensuring visibility of the valve position, crucial for emergency response and system maintenance. Each type serves to improve safety and operational reliability.</p></p>
