<p><strong>The Remolded Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre Cure</li><li>Mold Cure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The remolded tires market is primarily divided into two types: pre-cure and mold cure. Pre-cure tires use pre-vulcanized tread rubber, allowing for a quick and efficient application process where the tread is bonded to the tire. In contrast, mold cure tires are produced by curing the entire tire in a mold, resulting in potentially superior bonding and durability. Both types cater to distinct consumer needs, with pre-cure focusing on cost-effectiveness and mold cure emphasizing performance and longevity.</p></p>
