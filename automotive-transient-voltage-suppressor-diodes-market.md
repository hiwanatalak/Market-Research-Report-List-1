<p><p>The automotive transient voltage suppressor (TVS) diode market comprises two primary types: uni-polar and bi-polar TVS diodes. Uni-polar TVS diodes are designed to protect circuits from unidirectional voltage spikes, making them suitable for applications where the current flows in one direction. In contrast, bi-polar TVS diodes can handle bidirectional voltage transients, providing protection for circuits where current may flow in both directions. Both types are essential for safeguarding sensitive automotive electronics from voltage fluctuations and surges, ensuring reliable operation and longevity.</p></p>
