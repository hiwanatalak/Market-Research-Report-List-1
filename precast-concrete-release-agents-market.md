<p><p>Precast concrete release agents are essential for ensuring easy removal of concrete from molds without damage. The market is divided into oil-based and water-based types. Oil-based release agents provide a durable barrier, ideal for high-strength molds, often delivering better performance but may require solvents for cleanup. In contrast, water-based release agents are more environmentally friendly, easier to clean, and less hazardous, making them suitable for various applications. Each type serves specific needs based on mold materials and project requirements.</p></p>
