<p><strong>The Microbiological Safety Cabinet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class I Microbiological Safety Cabinets</li><li>Class II Microbiological Safety Cabinets</li><li>Class III Microbiological Safety Cabinets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microbiological safety cabinet market comprises three primary types: Class I, Class II, and Class III cabinets. Class I cabinets provide personnel protection and airflow containment but do not offer product protection. Class II cabinets are further categorized into types A1, A2, B1, and B2, ensuring both personnel and product protection through specialized airflow systems. Class III cabinets offer the highest level of safety, featuring sealed environments for handling hazardous materials and protecting users from exposure. Each type serves specific applications in laboratory settings.</p></p>
