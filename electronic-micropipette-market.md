<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Multi-channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic micropipette market is segmented into single-channel and multi-channel types. Single-channel micropipettes are designed for precise liquid transfer one sample at a time, ideal for applications requiring high accuracy and control. In contrast, multi-channel micropipettes allow for simultaneous dispensing across multiple wells, significantly enhancing throughput in tasks like high-throughput screening or PCR setup. While single-channel models prioritize precision, multi-channel options focus on efficiency, catering to diverse laboratory needs in research and diagnostics.</p></p>
