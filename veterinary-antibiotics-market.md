<p><p>The veterinary antibiotics market includes several key types. Tetracyclines are broad-spectrum antibiotics effective against various bacterial infections in animals. Penicillins are commonly used for treating infections, particularly in livestock. Macrolides have a unique chemical structure that targets respiratory pathogens. Sulphonamides are synthetic antibiotics widely used for their efficacy against a range of infections. Aminoglycosides are potent against serious bacterial infections but require careful use due to potential toxicity. Each type plays a critical role in animal health management and productivity.</p></p>
