<p><strong>The ArF Implant Resists Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Line Resist</li><li>Space Resist</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ArF implant resists market is categorized into two main types: line resist and space resist. Line resists are designed to protect features during the implant process for narrower, closely spaced patterns, ensuring precise definition and resolution. Conversely, space resists are used for broader gaps between features, allowing more significant spacing without compromising the integrity of the semiconductor device. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and fidelity of advanced lithographic processes in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
