<p><p>The automotive traction motor core market includes two primary types: permanent magnet motor cores and AC induction motor cores. Permanent magnet motor cores utilize embedded magnets to enhance efficiency and provide high torque at low speeds, making them ideal for electric vehicles. In contrast, AC induction motor cores operate using electromagnetic induction, typically found in hybrid applications. Both types cater to the growing demand for electric and hybrid vehicles, emphasizing performance, energy efficiency, and reduced emissions in automotive propulsion systems.</p></p>
