<p><p>The COVID-19 IgG/IgM Rapid Test Cassette market includes various types of diagnostic methods. Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDT) offer quick results through a simple procedure, identifying antibodies against the virus. Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) provides a more detailed analysis, using enzyme-linked antibodies to detect and quantify IgG and IgM levels. Neutralization Assays assess the functional ability of antibodies to neutralize the virus, providing insights into immune response and potential vaccine efficacy. Each method serves distinct diagnostic needs in the pandemic.</p></p>
