<p><p>The Home Non-Invasive Ventilator market for Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD) is categorized into two main types: Single Level Ventilators and Bilevel Ventilators. Single Level Ventilators deliver a constant airflow at a set pressure, ideal for patients needing straightforward support. In contrast, Bilevel Ventilators provide two different pressure levels—one for inhalation and another for exhalation—which enhances comfort and compliance, making them suitable for patients with more complex respiratory needs. Both types aim to improve patients’ quality of life at home.</p></p>
