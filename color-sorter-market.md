<p><ul><li>Chute-Type Color Sorters</li><li>Belt-Type Color Sorters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The color sorter market is primarily divided into two types: chute-type and belt-type sorters. Chute-type color sorters utilize a vertical funnel mechanism where materials are fed through a chute, allowing for efficient sorting based on color through optical sensors. In contrast, belt-type color sorters use a conveyor belt system to transport materials, providing a more continuous flow and greater flexibility in speed and handling capacity. Both types enhance quality control in various industries, particularly in food processing and agriculture.</p></p>
