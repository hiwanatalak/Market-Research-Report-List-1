<p><p>The angioplasty balloon market features various types of balloon catheters designed for specific therapeutic needs. Normal balloon catheters are standard devices used to dilate narrowed arteries. Drug-Eluting Balloon (DEB) catheters release medication to prevent restenosis post-procedure. Cutting balloon catheters incorporate sharp blades for precise arterial dilation, useful in tough lesions. Scoring balloon catheters feature scored surfaces to enhance vessel expansion with lower pressure, promoting safer and more effective outcomes in angioplasty procedures.</p></p>
