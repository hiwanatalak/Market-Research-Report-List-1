<p><strong>The Direct Adaptive Steering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PE-Steering</li><li>BM-Steering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct Adaptive Steering technology offers two primary market types: PE-Steering (Performance Enhanced Steering) and BM-Steering (Body Motion Steering). PE-Steering focuses on enhancing vehicle performance, allowing for dynamic adjustments to steering response based on driving conditions, improving stability and maneuverability. Conversely, BM-Steering enhances ride comfort and vehicle control by adjusting steering inputs relative to body movements, reducing the influence of road irregularities. Both systems aim to optimize driving experience, safety, and overall vehicle handling.</p></p>
