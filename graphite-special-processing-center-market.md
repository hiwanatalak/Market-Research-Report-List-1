<p><p>The Graphite Special Processing Center market is categorized into two main types: dry type and wet type. Dry type processing involves the use of dry grinding and milling techniques, which produce fine graphite used in applications like lubricants and batteries. Wet type processing, on the other hand, uses water and chemical mixtures to enhance particle separation and purification, resulting in high-purity graphite suitable for advanced applications such as fuel cells and electronic components. Each type serves distinct industry needs.</p></p>
