<p><p>Fine ceramics for electronics encompass specialized materials with unique properties. The ferroelectric market focuses on ceramics exhibiting spontaneous polarization, ideal for memory devices and capacitors. The piezoelectric market involves materials that generate electric charge in response to mechanical stress, crucial for sensors and actuators. Conversely, the pyroelectric market centers on materials that produce electric voltage when subjected to temperature changes, primarily used in infrared sensors and thermal imaging applications. Together, these markets cater to diverse electronic applications, enhancing functionality and performance.</p></p>
