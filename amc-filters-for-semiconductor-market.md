<p><p>AMC filters, crucial in the semiconductor market, utilize chemisorption filters made from activated carbon or aluminas to capture and eliminate chemical contaminants from the air. These materials adsorb harmful gases and vapors, ensuring a clean manufacturing environment. Bonded media panels, consisting of activated carbon formed into monolithic structures, offer enhanced durability and efficiency in filtering. Together, these technologies help maintain the stringent cleanliness standards required in semiconductor fabrication processes, protecting sensitive equipment and improving product quality.</p></p>
