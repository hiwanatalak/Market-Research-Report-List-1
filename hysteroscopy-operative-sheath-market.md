<p><p>The hysteroscopy operative sheath market is segmented into single-channel and double-channel types. Single-channel sheaths allow for the passage of instruments through one pathway, ideal for basic procedures. In contrast, double-channel sheaths provide two separate passages, enabling concurrent use of multiple instruments, which increases procedural efficiency and versatility. This differentiation caters to varying clinical needs, facilitating either straightforward examinations or more complex interventions depending on the sheath type utilized in hysteroscopic procedures.</p></p>
