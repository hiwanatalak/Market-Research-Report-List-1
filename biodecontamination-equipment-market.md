<p><strong>The Biodecontamination Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chamber Decontamination</li><li>Room Decontamination</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biodecontamination equipment encompasses various methods for eliminating pathogens from surfaces and air in controlled environments. Chamber decontamination involves sealed enclosures where materials or equipment are exposed to disinfectants like gaseous hydrogen peroxide or ozone, ensuring thorough microbial reduction. Room decontamination, on the other hand, addresses larger spaces, utilizing techniques such as aerosolized disinfectants or UV light to sanitize entire areas effectively. Both types are essential in healthcare, pharmaceuticals, and research facilities to maintain sterile conditions and prevent contamination.</p></p>
