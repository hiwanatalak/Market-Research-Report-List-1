<p><p>The veterinary respiratory monitoring devices market comprises various types, including spirometry, plethysmography, and others. Spirometry measures the volume and flow of air during breathing, providing insight into lung function. Plethysmography assesses changes in lung volume and thoracic pressure, offering a comprehensive view of respiratory health. Other devices may include pulse oximeters and capnometers that monitor oxygen saturation and carbon dioxide levels, respectively. These tools are essential for diagnosing and managing respiratory conditions in animals, enhancing veterinary care and outcomes.</p></p>
