Enhanced GPS (E-GPS) further refines location tracking by integrating additional data sources, ensuring precise localization even in challenging environments. Other market types include Wi-Fi positioning and RFID, enabling healthcare facilities to monitor assets, streamline workflows, and enhance patient safety.</p></p>
